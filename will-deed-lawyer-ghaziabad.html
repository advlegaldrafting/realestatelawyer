Documents Required for Will Deed

To draft and register a Will Deed, you need the following documents:

  • Aadhaar Card or valid ID proof
  • Passport-size photographs
  • Property ownership documents (movable & immovable)
  • Details of beneficiaries
  • Two witnesses with ID proof

Will Registration Process

The Will registration process involves booking an appointment with the Sub-Registrar office, visiting on the scheduled date, and completing verification. Our team manages the entire process, ensuring a smooth and hassle-free experience without repeated visits.

Although registration of a Will is not mandatory under Indian law, it is highly recommended as it adds legal authenticity and reduces the chances of disputes.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

What is a Will Deed?
A Will Deed is a legal document that specifies how a person’s property will be distributed after death.

Is Will registration mandatory?
No, but it is advisable for legal safety.

Who can make a Will?
Any person above 18 years with a sound mind.

Can a Will be changed?
Yes, it can be modified anytime during the lifetime.

Is stamp duty required?
No, stamp duty is not applicable on a Will in India.
