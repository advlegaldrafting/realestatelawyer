What is SMC Succession Certificate?

This is an important legal document that identifies the rightful heirs of a deceased person. It is required for property transfer, bank claims, insurance settlement, and updating official records.

Why Legal Heir Certificate is Important?

A legal heir certificate confirms the relationship between the deceased person and heirs and is necessary for legal ownership claims.

What is Property Mutation?

Property mutation is the process of updating ownership details in government records after transfer of ownership.

Common Issues in Property Mutation

Incomplete documentation, incorrect filing, and lack of legal understanding often cause delays.
